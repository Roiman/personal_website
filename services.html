How I work

Fractional Head of Product

I join your team on an ongoing basis — typically two to four days a week — and take on the Head of Product role properly. That means owning the roadmap, leading the product team, working with engineering and design, and giving you a senior voice in the room for strategic decisions. It's the real thing, not a consultant at arm's length.

Product Advisory

If you already have product leadership in place but want an experienced outside perspective, I work with founders and PMs in a lighter advisory capacity. That might be a regular call to work through strategic questions, a sounding board for big decisions, or coaching for a PM who's growing into a leadership role.

One-off engagements

Sometimes you just need focused help on one thing. I run discovery sprints to clarify what your customers actually need, roadmap reviews to pressure-test your priorities, and team assessments to help you understand where your product function is strong and where it's not. These are typically one to four weeks.

Not sure which fits? Drop me a message and we'll figure it out.
